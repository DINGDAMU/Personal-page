Biography

Damu Ding received his B.Sc and M.Sc in telecommunications engineering from Politecnico di Torino, Italy and the University of Trento, Italy, respectively. In 2016 he joined CREATE-NET Research Center, Italy as a software developer working on the European 5G-Crosshaul project. He is currently a Ph.D student co-tutored by Fondazione Bruno Kessler, CREATE-NET Research Center, Italy and the University of Bologna, Italy. His major research interests include SDN, network monitoring and anomaly detection.
