Biography

Damu Ding (Student Member, IEEE) received the B.Sc. degree in telecommunications engineering from the Politecnico di Torino, Italy, and the M.Sc. degree in telecommunications engineering from the University of Trento, Italy. He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree co-tutored by Fondazione Bruno Kessler, Italy, and the University of Bologna, Italy. His major research interests include SDN, network monitoring and anomaly detection.
